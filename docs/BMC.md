# Business Model Canvas (BMC) for github-skills-map

## Value Proposition
### Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
- **Personalized developer skills map**: A visual representation of a developer's recent coding activity and skills, leveraging GitHub data.
- **Data-driven insights**: Identify strengths, weaknesses, and areas for improvement in coding skills.
- **Competitive advantage**: Stand out in the job market with a unique, data-backed skills showcase.

### Key Benefits
- **Career growth**: Enhance job prospects and career advancement opportunities.
- **Skill development**: Identify areas for improvement and focus on skill development.
- **Community engagement**: Connect with like-minded developers and join relevant communities.

## Customer Segments
### Primary Target Market
- **Developers**: Software engineers, programmers, and coders who use GitHub for their projects.
- **Career seekers**: Job seekers, students, and professionals looking to enhance their career prospects.

### Secondary Target Market
- **Educational institutions**: Universities, colleges, and coding boot camps that want to provide students with a skills assessment tool.
- **Recruiters and hiring managers**: Companies and individuals looking to assess candidate skills and potential.

## Channels
### Primary Channels
- **Web application**: A user-friendly web interface for generating and sharing skills maps.
- **GitHub integration**: Seamless integration with GitHub to access user data and activity.

### Secondary Channels
- **Social media**: Promote the platform through social media channels (Twitter, LinkedIn, GitHub).
- **Content marketing**: Create informative blog posts, videos, and tutorials to showcase the platform's value.

## Revenue Streams
### Primary Revenue Streams
- **Subscription-based model**: Offer users a monthly or annual subscription to access premium features.
- **Advertising**: Display targeted, non-intrusive ads on the platform.

### Secondary Revenue Streams
- **Data analytics**: Sell anonymized and aggregated data insights to educational institutions and companies.
- **Custom solutions**: Offer customized skills map solutions for educational institutions and companies.

## Cost Structure
### Fixed Costs
- **Development and maintenance**: Ongoing development and maintenance of the platform.
- **Infrastructure**: Server costs, database management, and other infrastructure expenses.

### Variable Costs
- **Marketing and advertising**: Expenses related to promoting the platform and attracting users.
- **Customer support**: Costs associated with providing customer support and assistance.

## Key Resources
- **Development team**: Experienced developers and engineers to maintain and improve the platform.
- **GitHub API access**: Secure access to GitHub data and API.
- **Server infrastructure**: Reliable and scalable server infrastructure to host the platform.

## Key Activities
- **Platform development**: Ongoing development and improvement of the platform.
- **Marketing and promotion**: Promote the platform through various channels and activities.
- **Customer support**: Provide excellent customer support and assistance.

## Key Partners
- **GitHub**: Collaborate with GitHub to ensure seamless integration and access to user data.
- **Educational institutions**: Partner with educational institutions to provide customized skills map solutions.
- **Companies**: Collaborate with companies to offer customized skills map solutions and data analytics.
