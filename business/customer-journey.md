 # Customer-Journey.md

## Aware

### Trigger Event
- Developer realizes the need to showcase their coding activity and skills to potential employers or clients.
- Developer encounters a job posting requiring evidence of recent coding activity and skills.

### Friction Points
- Difficulty in manually tracking and organizing coding activity across multiple repositories.
- Lack of a standardized format for presenting coding activity and skills.
- Time-consuming process of manually creating a portfolio or resume.

### User Emotions
- Frustration at the time-consuming and laborious process of tracking and showcasing coding activity.
- Anxiety about the potential consequences of not being able to effectively demonstrate coding skills.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Seamless integration with popular coding platforms like GitHub, Bitbucket, or GitLab.
- Automatic tracking and organization of coding activity.
- Personalized and visually appealing presentation of coding activity and skills.

### Metric
- Number of developers reached through marketing efforts.

## Consider

### Trigger Event
- Developer becomes aware of github-skills-map through marketing efforts (social media, job boards, etc.).
- Developer conducts online research to compare github-skills-map with other similar platforms.

### Friction Points
- Lack of understanding of how github-skills-map works.
- Concerns about the platform's security and privacy policies.
- Uncertainty about the platform's effectiveness in showcasing coding activity and skills.

### User Emotions
- Interest in finding an efficient solution to track and showcase coding activity.
- Skepticism about the platform's ability to meet their needs.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Clear and concise explanations of how github-skills-map works.
- Transparent security and privacy policies.
- Testimonials and case studies demonstrating the platform's effectiveness.

### Metric
- Number of developers visiting the github-skills-map website.
- Time spent on the website by potential customers.

## Try

### Trigger Event
- Developer signs up for a free trial of github-skills-map.
- Developer connects their GitHub account to github-skills-map.

### Friction Points
- Difficulty in setting up the connection with GitHub.
- Uncertainty about the accuracy of the generated skills map.
- Frustration with a slow or unresponsive platform.

### User Emotions
- Excitement at the prospect of easily tracking and showcasing coding activity.
- Frustration with any technical issues encountered during setup or use.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Seamless and easy setup process.
- Accurate and comprehensive skills map.
- Fast and responsive platform.

### Metric
- Number of free trials started.
- Number of free trials converted to paid subscriptions.

## Adopt

### Trigger Event
- Developer decides to purchase a paid subscription to github-skills-map.
- Developer begins using github-skills-map regularly to track and showcase coding activity.

### Friction Points
- Difficulty in understanding how to use all the features of github-skills-map.
- Frustration with any technical issues encountered during use.

### User Emotions
- Satisfaction with the ability to easily track and showcase coding activity.
- Gratitude for the time saved by using github-skills-map.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Comprehensive onboarding and support resources.
- Regular updates and improvements to the platform.
- Personalized recommendations for improving coding skills.

### Metric
- Number of paid subscriptions.
- Average length of time a customer remains a paid subscriber.

## Expand

### Trigger Event
- Developer becomes comfortable using github-skills-map and wants to explore additional features.
- Developer recommends github-skills-map to other developers.

### Friction Points
- Difficulty in understanding how to use advanced features.
- Frustration with any technical issues encountered during use.

### User Emotions
- Excitement at the prospect of further improving their coding skills.
- Satisfaction with the ability to easily recommend github-skills-map to others.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Comprehensive onboarding and support resources for advanced features.
- Incentives for referring other developers to github-skills-map.
- Regular updates and improvements to the platform based on customer feedback.

### Metric
- Number of active users.
- Number of new customers acquired through referrals.
