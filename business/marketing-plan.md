### 2. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)  

| Persona | Daily Job | Pain Point | Approx. Budget (annual) |
|---------|-----------|------------|--------------------------|
| **Lena “The Job‑Seeker”** – Front‑end Engineer, 27, San Francisco | Writes React/TS components, applies to 10+ jobs/week, needs a portfolio that stands out. | Recruiters skim resumes; GitHub links are noisy. Wants a concise visual proof of recent work. | **$2,500** (personal dev tools budget) |
| **Marco “The Team Lead”** – Backend Lead, 35, Berlin | Oversees 6 engineers, conducts weekly sprint demos, needs to allocate tasks based on skill gaps. | Hard to see who has recent experience with a given tech stack without digging through PRs. | **$7,500** (team tooling budget) |
| **Priya “The Learning Coach”** – Developer Advocate, 32, Bangalore | Runs workshops, curates learning paths, promotes community members. | Needs a quick way to showcase a learner’s progress to sponsors and peers. | **$4,000** (training & community budget) |

*Total addressable market (TAM) estimate*: ~120k developers in the 25‑40 age bracket across North America & EU with > 100 commits/mo → **$300M** potential ARR.

### 3. Channels & CAC Estimate  

| Channel | Tactics (first 90 days) | Estimated CAC |
|---------|------------------------|---------------|
| **Developer Communities (Dev.to, Hacker News, Reddit r/coding)** | Sponsored posts, AMA with founder, free‑tier “skill‑map” giveaway (limit 5 k users) | **$12** per paid conversion |
| **Product‑Led Growth (PLG) – Free‑to‑Premium SaaS** | In‑app upsell after 5 maps generated, referral credit ($5 per friend) | **$9** per paid conversion |
| **Partner Integrations (GitHub Marketplace, VS Code Extension)** | Co‑marketing with GitHub Actions, bundle with CI tools, cross‑promo webinars | **$15** per paid conversion |

*Assumption*: 30 % of free users convert to paid within 30 days; average LTV ≈ $360 (annual $300 + churn buffer).

### 5. Launch Milestones  

| Milestone | Date | Deliverable |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| **D‑30** | Day ‑30 | • Finalised MVP (public repo, CI pipeline) <br>• Landing page + email capture (goal: 5 k sign‑ups) <br>• Beta invite list (2 k devs) |

### 6. Success Metrics  

| Metric | Target (by D +90) |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Daily Active Users (DAU)** | **6,000** |
| **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)** | **$45,000** |
| **Conversion Rate (free → paid)** | **30 %** |
| **Churn (30‑day)** | **< 5 %** |
| **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** | **≥ 45** |
| **Referral Viral Coefficient** | **≥ 1.2** |

*If any metric falls short by > 15 % at D +90, trigger a 2‑week sprint to optimise the under‑performing channel or pricing tier.*
